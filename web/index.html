ATL — Fatigue i
🔥 ATL — Acute Training Load
Short-term training stress from the past ~7 days. Spikes quickly after hard sessions.
ATLtoday = ATLprev + (load − ATLprev) ÷ 7
High ATL = high recent fatigue. Drops fast with rest.
CTL — Fitness i
📈 CTL — Chronic Training Load
Long-term fitness base built over ~42 days of consistent training.
CTLtoday = CTLprev + (load − CTLprev) ÷ 42
Rises slowly with sustained training. Takes weeks to build.
